## Strategy

### Entry Conditions

Requires confluence of 3+ factors:

1. **Trend Alignment:** Price must be trading above 200-day moving average (bullish context) OR showing clear oversold conditions on daily RSI (<30) with bullish divergence
2. **Support/Resonance:** Entry near established support levels (previous consolidation zones, Fibonacci retracements 0.5-0.618, or psychological price levels)
3. **Volume Confirmation:** Increasing volume on bullish moves, decreasing volume on pullbacks
4. **Fundamental Catalyst (optional but preferred):** Positive news, network growth, or macro conditions favoring crypto

Special setups:
- **Pullback to Value:** After strong uptrend, enter on 20-30% pullback to key support with volume drying up
- **Breakout Confirmation:** Enter on breakout from multi-week consolidation with volume surge 2x+ average
- **Oversold Reversal:** RSI <30 on daily with bullish divergence and MACD histogram contracting

### Exit Conditions

- **Take Profit:**
  - First target: 50% of position at +8-12% gain (bank profits, reduce risk)
  - Second target: 25% at +15-20% gain
  - Final target: 25% at +25-35% gain or trailing stop hit

- **Stop Loss:**
  - Hard stop at -5% below entry (strict discipline)
  - If stopped out 3 times in a month, pause trading for 2 weeks to review strategy

- **Trailing Method:**
  - After hitting first target, trail stop at breakeven
  - After 15% gain, trail at 10% below highest price (soft trailing stop)
  - Uses 20-day EMA as dynamic trailing stop for remaining position

## Technical Indicators

### Primary Indicators

- **Moving Averages:**
  - 20-day EMA: Short-term trend and dynamic support
  - 50-day SMA: Medium-term trend
  - 200-day SMA: Long-term trend (bullish market filter)

- **RSI (14):**
  - Overbought: >70 on daily (consider taking profits)
  - Oversold: <30 on daily (look for reversal setups)
  - Bullish/Bearish divergence between price and RSI

- **MACD (12, 26, 9):**
  - MACD line crosses above/below signal line
  - Histogram contraction/expansion
  - Histogram divergence from price

- **Volume:**
  - Confirms breakouts (volume surge 2x+ average)
  - Drying up volume on pullbacks (healthy correction)
  - OBV (On-Balance Volume) for trend confirmation

### Secondary Indicators

- **Bollinger Bands (20, 2):** Identify volatility extremes and mean reversion points
- **Fibonacci Retracements:** Identify support/resistance levels (0.382, 0.5, 0.618)
- **ATR (14):** Measure volatility for position sizing and stop placement
